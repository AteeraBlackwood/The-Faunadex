The Faunadex

Heatmor vs. Durant

Heatmor

Heatmor, the Anteater pokemon is a fire type based on of the giant anteater. Durant are the preferred prey of Heatmor. It burns through Durant's steel bodies with its tongue and consumes their insides. It uses its tail to absorb the air in order to keep its fire burning.

Giant Anteater

The giant anteater is an insectivorous mammal native to Central and South America. They can grow up to 217cm in length. They feed primarily on ants and termites. Their claws are strong enough to break through concrete. Mother anteaters carry their offspring on their backs until weaning them.

Durant

Durant , the Iron Ant pokemon is a bug and steel type based on the trap-jaw ant. They attack in groups, covering themselves in steel armor to protect themselves from Heatmor. With their large mandibles, these Pokémon can crunch their way through rock. It will attack swiftly and aggressively if bothered, but will not pursue intruders once outside of its colony.

Trap-Jaw Ant

The trap-jaw ants are a family of omnivorous ants found in the tropics and subtropics throughout the world. The species have a pair of large, straight mandibles capable of opening 180°. These jaws are locked in place by an internal mechanism, and can snap shut on prey or objects when sensory hairs on the inside of the mandibles are touched. The ants also use their jaws to catapult both intruders and themselves.
