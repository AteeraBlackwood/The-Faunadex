Heliolisk

Heliolisk, the Generator Pokemon, is an electric normal type based on the Australian Frilled Lizard. It evolves from Helioptile when exposed to a sun stone. Heliolisk can generate sufficient electricity to power a skyscraper, simply by flaring open its frill.

Frilled Lizard

The frilled lizard is endemic to northern Australia and southern New Guinea. It spends the majority of its time in trees and mostly eats insects and small vertebrates. Frilled lizards stand on their back legs when they raise their frills to scare another animal. They also run on their hind legs when afraid. The species' main predators are eagles, owls, snakes, dingoes, quolls, and larger lizards such as goannas.
