Orchid Mantis

The Orchid Mantis is a mantis from the tropical forests of Southeast Asia. This species mimics parts of the orchid flower. The toothed front pair is used as in other mantises for grasping prey. Mantises predate on larger pollinating insects. The females, like other mantis species, eat the male after mating. They guard the eggs until they hatch.
